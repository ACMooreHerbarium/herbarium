Welcome to the University of South Carolina Herbarium! Providing services and educational outreach to the public and researchers alike, the University of South Carolina Herbarium is "The Peoples' Herbarium". At just over 100,000 dried plant specimens, the University of South Carolina Herbarium houses the largest collection in the state of South Carolina.
