Mystery Plants

Streaming Video

SCETV's popular broadcast Making it Grow! hosts a "Mystery Plant" segment each Tuesday night at 7PM. The "Mystery Plant" segment appears approximately 15 to 20 minutes into each episode. To view previously aired episodes please click here. Earlier episodes are available at the bottom of their page.

Past Mystery Plants

The "Mystery Plant" appears each week in a number of newspapers in South Carolina, and elsewhere in the Southeast. This series is provided to newspapers, WITHOUT CHARGE, as a way of introducing the public to interesting and/or unusual plants, and as a way of advertising the Herbarium's free plant identification service. If you enjoy reading these installments, please consider asking your local newspaper to consider running them. If you are a newspaper editor and are interested in this series, please contact us at your convenience.
