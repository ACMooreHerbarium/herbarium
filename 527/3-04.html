FLESHY FRUITS

drupe (one-seeded, pericarp of distinct layers: exocarp, mesocarp, endocarp)
      ex: peach, plum, cherry, avocado

berry (more than one seed, no hard endocarp)
      ex: blueberry, tomato, grape

hesperidium (only found in the citrus family - leathery rind with oil-bearing glands, and filled with juicy, modified trichomes)
      ex: tangerine

pepo - a kind of berry produced by the cucumber family, with a leathery rind
      ex: cucmber, pumpkin, bottle gourd

accesory fruit (additional tissue ripens and beomes fleshy as the ovary matures
- pome (hypanthium becomes enlarged and fleshy)
      ex: apple, pear
