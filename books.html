Wishlist

The A. C. Moore Herbarium strives to provide researchers and the public with the best possible botanical information. Without a reliable reference library this would not be possible. If you would like to help the A. C. Moore Herbarium Botanical Library grow, please consider making a book donation. To make a book donation, select a title from the list below. You will be directed to Biblio.com. Add the title to your cart. When you are ready to pay, please have your selection shipped to:

    Biological Sciences
