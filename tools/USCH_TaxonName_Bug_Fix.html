2009-03-25 Eight Duplicate Names Removed

In response to Steve Ginzbarg of University of Alabama, 8 duplicate names were removed:

  • Mammal
  • Plant
  • Reptile
  • Fish
  • Amphibian
  • Bird
  • Insect
  • Invertebrate

2009-03-26 Seven Orders Moved to Hamamelididae

In response to Steve Ginzbarg of University of Alabama, 7 Orders were moved from the incorrectly spelled subclass Hamamelidae to the correctly spelled subclass Hamamelididae:

  • Casuarinales
  • Fagales
  • Hamamelidales
  • Juglandales
  • Leitneriales
  • Myricales
  • Urticales
